Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to the 'My Plan' section on the mobile app, and selecting 'Cancel My Subscription'.

I've lost my password. How do I reset it?

You can reset your password by clicking 'Forgot My Password' upon sign in, and then it will send a link to create a new password on your email.

Is SoundWave available on Windows?

Yes! We are compatible with the following software: MacOS, Windows, IOS, Android.
